Roman Culture

I am fascinated by roman culture and history, specifically the Twelve Tables, as I believe it is the foundation of modern legal systems. The Twelve Tables represent a significant step in the development of law and governance, emphasizing the importance of written laws and the rights of citizens. For the first time, the common man had a written document to reference when defending their rights.
